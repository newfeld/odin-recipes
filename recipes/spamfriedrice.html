Spam Fried Rice

Recipe Description

Fried rice is a classic easy and affordable meal, but this recipe in particular is made even easier and more affordable with Spam and any vegetables you can find in your fridge. I find myself making this meal when I need to use up any and all ingredients left in the fridge. Another essential part of the college experience means using the least amount of dishes possible, and the fact that this dish only requires a single pan or wok makes this the ultimate student meal.

Ingredients

  • Spam
  • Day-old-rice
  • Eggs
  • Green onions
  • Frozen veggie mix
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Desired Seasonings

Steps

  1. Dice Spam into small pieces.
  2. Chop up green onions
  3. Cook Spam in a hot pan with oil, add some soy sauce to get absorbed into the spam for flavor.
  4. Take Spam out of the pan and cook eggs until scrambled.
  5. Add your refrigerated rice, frozen veggies, and Spam back to the pan and cook until rice is soft again.
  6. Pour some soy sauce into the mixture.
  7. Continue stirring the fried rice mixture, adding seasonings and soy sauce to desired amount and flavor.
  8. Before plating, add some sesame oil and mix it in.
  9. Plate the fried rice and add green onions to garnish.
  10. Enjoy.
