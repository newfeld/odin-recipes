Kimchi with Chicken and Rice

Description

This is an age old budget meal enjoyed by students, meal preppers, athletes, and anybody who needs a quick meal due to its simplicity, adaptability, and high nutritional value. I like this dish because you can cook the chicken any way you desire with any seasonings and sauces you want and it will pretty much taste good no matter what. I like to add kimchi to the side for some color and to make my mother feel better about my dieting habits at school. This recipe is how I typically prepare this dish and will utilize some of Trader Joes' magical ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Boneless Chicken thighs
  • White rice
  • Trader Ming's General Tso's Stir Fry Sauce
  • Trader Ming's Sweet Chili Sauce
  • Kimchi(Homemade if you wanna be extra)
  • Salt, pepper, and any other desired seasonings(I like garlic powder and smoked paprika)

Steps

  1. Wash rice and start cooking in a rice cooker so that it will be done when the chicken is finished.
  2. Slice chicken thighs into bite sized pieces.
  3. Place chicken in a hot pan with oil, seasoning with salt, pepper, smoked paprika, and garlic powder.
  4. Cook chicken for about 6 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Pour stir fry sauce and sweet chili sauce on chicken and cook for another 5 minutes.
  6. Plate the cooked chicken with rice on the side, and add the desired amount of kimchi.
  7. All done, enjoy dinner with a Youtube video.
