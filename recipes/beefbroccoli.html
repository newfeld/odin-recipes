Beef and Broccoli

Recipe Description

Can't afford a trip to Panda Express for the third time this week? Well, this is the perfect recipe for you. Combine two simple ingredients to make this classic Asian dish in the comfort of your tiny college home. Packed with flavor, this meal will also give you a protein boost to help you work up the courage to walk down those steps to the very front of the lecture hall.

Ingredients

  • Thin-sliced strips of top sirloin steak
  • Broccoli florets
  • Soy sauce
  • sesame oil
  • ginger
  • minced garlic
  • sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Fry broccoli in a hot pan with oil, seasoning with salt and pepper.
  2. Remove broccoli from the pan and add oil.
  3. Cook steak strips in a searing hot pan for 6-8 minutes.
  4. While steak is cooking, make the stir fry sauce using soy sauce, sesame oil, minced garlic, ginger, and add some chili sauce if some heat is desired.
  5. After steak has been cooking for 6-8 minutes, add stir fry sauce and cook for an additional 5 minutes.
  6. Finally, add broccoli and stir the mixture until it looks good(you're a college student, not a Michellin star chef).
  7. Add your broccoli and beef to a bowl of white rice and garnish with green onions and sesame seeds to make it look good.
  8. Feast.
